To Someone Special

How are you? I hope you're doing well. Today, I want to tell you something from my heart— I’m always with you, and I will always try to be by your side. You may not realize it, but my thoughts are always with you. I can never forget you, because you hold a special place in my heart. I’ll always try to be someone close to you, because even if distance is possible, staying away from you isn’t for me. Maybe I don’t mean much in your life, but I’ll never stop trying—to be there for you, to be part of your happiness and pain, even if silently.

— Your Unknown Friend
